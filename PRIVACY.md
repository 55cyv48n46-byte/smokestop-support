# SmokeStop Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

## Data Collection
SmokeStop does not collect, store, or share any personal data. 
All information you enter (quit date, smoking habits, streak) 
is stored locally on your device only.

## Subscriptions
SmokeStop Pro is an auto-renewable subscription managed by Apple. 
Subscription management and cancellation is handled through your 
Apple ID settings or within the app.

## Third-Party Services
SmokeStop uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. 
RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. 
See RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## Contact
For questions or support: your@email.com

## Changes
We may update this policy. Continued use of the app 
means you accept any changes.
